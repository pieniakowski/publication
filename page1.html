The assignment had the objective of learning to associate. The object I wanted to use was underpants. We use this to cover ourselves up, because we are ashamed of our sexuality and because of society. I went back and was inspired by some old classic paintings of Adam and Eve, where it all began according to the Bible. So I searched two models who were qualified to play Adam and Eve. I took pictures of them, while they posed as in the classic paintings. I gradually covered them up, from no coverage to more and more coverage with leaves and eventually wearing underpants. The plants I used were pictures and scans from the botanical garden in Ghent. That is how I tried to narrate the story of Adam and Eve.

This album is called ‘Trip into the body’ by Johan Timman. Each song of the album represents a part of the body (eg.: the lungs, the heart,…). Johan Timman was obsessed with analogue synthesizers and used these synths to produce all of his music. I combined the content of the music with the way that electronic music is made. The front is a picture I took of a kidney dialysis machine and focussed on the wiring. On the back a moog is depicted with the crisscross wiring. The threads appear to be running into one another from the front to the back. On the plate itself, on the inside, are the organs of the body.
